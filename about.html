Hey there! My name is Doyeon (/doh-yeon/)

I'm a UI/UX Storytelling and Designer passionate about creating meaningful and intuitive digital experiences.

Originally from Korea, I was raised in the DMV area and now call Philadelphia home as I pursue a B.S. in User Experience and Interaction Design at Drexel University.

My journey into design is driven by my love for blending art with functionality. I believe great design is more than just beautiful—it’s purposeful and empathetic to the people who use it.

Beyond design, I’m drawn to capturing the beauty in the world through my camera lens. Whether I’m sketching new ideas, dancing, playing chess, or working on a photography project, I’m always exploring new ways to express stories.
